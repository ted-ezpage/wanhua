艋舺龍山寺 | Longshan Temple

台北最古老的寺廟之一 | One of Taipei's Oldest Temples

🏮 中文介紹

艋舺龍山寺創建於1738年，是台北市最古老、最著名的寺廟之一，也是台灣重要的文化資產。這座寺廟融合了佛教、道教和民間信仰，主要供奉觀世音菩薩。

龍山寺以其精美的傳統建築藝術聞名，包括華麗的雕刻、彩繪和剪粘工藝。寺廟的建築風格展現了閩南傳統工藝的精髓，每一個細節都充滿了文化意涵。

這裡不僅是信徒祈福的聖地，也是遊客體驗台灣傳統文化的重要景點。每日都有許多虔誠的信徒前來膜拜，香火鼎盛，充滿了濃厚的宗教氛圍。

🏮 English Introduction

Longshan Temple, built in 1738, is one of Taipei's oldest and most famous temples, and an important cultural heritage site in Taiwan. This temple combines Buddhism, Taoism, and folk beliefs, primarily worshipping Guanyin (Goddess of Mercy).

The temple is renowned for its exquisite traditional architectural art, including ornate carvings, paintings, and ceramic decorations. The architectural style showcases the essence of Fujian traditional craftsmanship, with every detail filled with cultural significance.

This is not only a sacred place for believers to pray for blessings, but also an important attraction for tourists to experience traditional Taiwanese culture. Every day, many devout believers come to worship, with incense burning brightly, creating a strong religious atmosphere.

華西街夜市 | Huaxi Street Night Market

台北最具特色的夜市之一 | One of Taipei's Most Distinctive Night Markets

🍜 中文介紹

華西街夜市位於萬華區，是台北市最具歷史的夜市之一，成立於1962年。這個夜市以其獨特的「蛇肉」料理聞名，吸引了無數好奇的遊客前來體驗。

夜市內販售各式各樣的傳統台灣小吃，包括滷肉飯、麵線、胡椒餅等經典美食。這裡保留了最原始的夜市風貌，充滿了濃厚的懷舊氛圍。

華西街夜市不僅是美食天堂，也是體驗台灣庶民文化的最佳場所。夜晚時分，霓虹燈閃爍，人聲鼎沸，展現了台北夜生活的獨特魅力。

🍜 English Introduction

Huaxi Street Night Market, located in Wanhua District, is one of Taipei's most historic night markets, established in 1962. This night market is famous for its unique "snake meat" cuisine, attracting countless curious tourists to experience it.

The night market sells a variety of traditional Taiwanese snacks, including braised pork rice, oyster vermicelli, pepper buns, and other classic delicacies. It maintains the most original night market atmosphere, filled with a strong nostalgic ambiance.

Huaxi Street Night Market is not only a food paradise but also the best place to experience Taiwanese folk culture. At night, neon lights flicker and voices buzz, showcasing the unique charm of Taipei's nightlife.

遊覽建議 | Travel Tips

🚇 交通方式

捷運：搭乘板南線至龍山寺站，1號出口步行約3分鐘即可到達龍山寺，華西街夜市就在附近。

公車：搭乘多線公車至龍山寺站下車。

⏰ 最佳遊覽時間

龍山寺：上午6:00-晚上10:00，建議避開週末人潮。

華西街夜市：晚上6:00-深夜12:00，晚上8-10點最熱鬧。

💡 遊覽小貼士

• 參觀龍山寺時請保持肅靜，尊重宗教文化

• 夜市內攤販眾多，建議先逛一圈再決定用餐

• 可以一次遊覽兩個景點，體驗傳統與現代的結合

🚇 Transportation

MRT: Take the Bannan Line to Longshan Temple Station, 3-minute walk from Exit 1 to reach Longshan Temple, with Huaxi Street Night Market nearby.

Bus: Take various bus lines to Longshan Temple Station.

⏰ Best Visiting Time

Longshan Temple: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM, avoid weekend crowds.

Huaxi Street Night Market: 6:00 PM - 12:00 AM, busiest from 8-10 PM.

💡 Travel Tips

• Keep quiet and respect religious culture when visiting the temple

• Browse the night market first before deciding where to eat

• Visit both attractions in one trip to experience traditional and modern fusion
