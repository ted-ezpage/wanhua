週五夜晚的艋舺探險 Friday Night in Monga

一趟穿梭於心靈與味蕾的旅程 A Journey for the Soul and the Palate

第一站 | Stop 1

艋舺龍山寺 Lungshan Temple

文化背景介紹 Cultural Introduction

  • 悠久的歷史：建於1738年，是台北最古老的寺廟之一，不僅是宗教中心，也是早期移民社群的議事場所。 A Rich History: Founded in 1738, it's one of Taipei's oldest temples, serving as both a religious and community center for early Chinese settlers.
  • 神明的集合：主神為觀世音菩薩(Guanyin)，同時供奉超過100尊來自佛教、道教及民間信仰的神明。 A Mix of Deities: The main deity is Guanyin (the Bodhisattva of Compassion), but it houses over 100 other gods and goddesses from Buddhist, Taoist, and folk traditions.
  • 建築之美：精美的中國宮殿式建築，有著全台灣唯一的銅鑄龍柱，屋頂上的剪黏藝術更是華麗絕倫。 Architectural Beauty: A masterpiece of classical Chinese architecture, featuring the only pair of bronze dragon pillars in Taiwan and intricate rooftop decorations.
  • 浴火重生的奇蹟：二戰時正殿被毀，但觀音神像卻完好無損，使其更添傳奇色彩。 Miracle of Resilience: The main hall was destroyed during a WWII air raid, but the statue of Guanyin miraculously survived unscathed, cementing its sacred status.

參拜禮儀小提示 A Guide to Temple Etiquette

  • 右進左出：從龍門進、虎門出，象徵趨吉避凶。記得不要踩踏門檻！ Enter Right, Exit Left: Enter through the "Dragon Door" (right) and exit through the "Tiger Door" (left) for good fortune. Avoid stepping on the threshold.
  • 保持尊敬：雙手合十，或點香參拜。最重要的是保持一顆尊敬與欣賞的心。 Be Respectful: You can pray with your palms together or with incense. The key is to be respectful and appreciative of the sacred space.
  • 觀察在地文化：可以看看信眾如何擲筊(moon blocks)和求籤(fortune sticks)與神明溝通，非常有趣。 Observe Local Culture: Watch how locals use "moon blocks" (bwa bwei) and "fortune sticks" (chim) to communicate with the deities. It's fascinating!

第二站 | Stop 2

華西街夜市 & 小王煮瓜 Huaxi St. Night Market & Hsiao Wang Chih Kua

從蛇街到米其林美食 From Snake Alley to Michelin Bib Gourmand

從龍山寺步行五分鐘，即可抵達台灣第一座觀光夜市。華西街過去以「蛇街」聞名，現已轉型為美食天堂。今晚的目標是連續多年榮獲米其林必比登推介的「小王煮瓜」！ A 5-minute walk from the temple brings us to Taiwan's first-ever tourist night market. Once known as "Snake Alley," Huaxi Street is now a foodie paradise. Our destination tonight is "Hsiao Wang Chih Kua," a legendary eatery awarded the Michelin Bib Gourmand for multiple years.

必點菜色推薦 Must-Try Dishes

  • 香菇魯肉飯：被譽為「黑金魯肉飯」，滷汁顏色偏深，鹹中帶甜，入口即化的肥美豬肉是其靈魂。 Braised Pork Rice: Known for its signature dark, savory-sweet soy sauce. The fatty pork melts in your mouth and is incredibly flavorful.
  • 清湯瓜仔肉：招牌湯品。以豬後腿肉與醃漬花瓜燉煮，湯頭清甜爽口，與濃郁的魯肉飯是絕配。 Pork and Pickled Cucumber Soup: The signature soup. It's a clear, light, and sweet broth made with pork and pickled cucumbers—a perfect complement to the rich rice dish.
  • 各式小菜：滷豆腐、滷鴨蛋、滷白菜都吸飽了店家特製的滷汁，非常入味。 Side Dishes: Try the braised tofu, duck egg, or cabbage. They are all soaked in the same delicious, signature braising sauce.

實用資訊 Practical Info

  • 交通：搭乘捷運板南線 (藍線) 至「龍山寺站」，從 1 號出口出站。 Transportation: Take the MRT Bannan Line (Blue Line) to "Longshan Temple Station" and use Exit 1.
  • 穿著：建議穿著舒適的鞋子。進入寺廟時，請穿著得體，避免過於暴露。 What to Wear: Comfortable shoes are a must. For the temple, please dress modestly (cover shoulders and knees).
  • 提醒：夜市大多只收現金，記得帶夠錢。保持好奇心，玩得開心！ Tips: Bring cash, as most night market vendors don't accept cards. Be curious and have fun!

祝您有個美好的夜晚！ Enjoy your wonderful evening!
