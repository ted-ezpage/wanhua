STOP 1

艋舺龍山寺
Lungshan Temple

踏入台北的心臟，感受近300年的歷史脈動。這裡不只是一座寺廟，更是一間「神的百貨公司」，是信仰與藝術的完美結晶。

Step into the heart of old Taipei and feel nearly 300 years of history. This isn't just a temple; it's a "department store of deities" and a stunning fusion of faith and art.

✨觀賞重點 / Don't Miss

  • 🐲

    銅鑄龍柱：全台唯一的銅製龍柱，盤踞在正殿前，氣勢非凡。

    Bronze Dragon Pillars: The only pair in Taiwan, guarding the main hall with majestic power.

  • 🙏

    月下老人：想求姻緣嗎？這裡的月老極富盛名，是年輕人的熱門朝聖地！

    The Matchmaker God: Looking for love? The "Yue Lao" here is famously effective and a popular spot for singles!

  • 🎨

    屋頂藝術：抬頭看！屋頂上的彩色剪黏和交趾陶，訴說著一則又一則的傳奇故事。

    Rooftop Artistry: Look up! The colorful mosaic-like figures tell countless legendary stories.

💡參拜體驗四步驟 / A 4-Step Guide

  • ➡️

    入龍喉：從右邊的龍門進入，象徵一步躍龍門。

    Enter the Dragon: Come in through the right gate (Dragon Gate) for good fortune.

  • 💧

    淨心淨手：在入口旁的瀑布前靜心，感受水的清涼。

    Cleanse & Purify: Pause by the waterfall at the entrance to clear your mind and hands.

  • 🙇

    誠心參拜：先拜天公，再拜主神觀世音菩薩。但心誠則靈最重要。

    Pray with Heart: Start with the Sky God, then the main deity Guanyin. A sincere heart is what matters most.

  • ⬅️

    出虎口：從左邊的虎門離開，帶走厄運，留下好運。

    Exit the Tiger: Leave through the left gate (Tiger Gate) to ward off misfortune.

STOP 2

華西街 & 小王煮瓜
Huaxi St. & Hsiao Wang

STOP 2

華西街 & 小王煮瓜
Huaxi St. & Hsiao Wang

告別莊嚴，我們走入人間煙火。穿過標誌性的紅色牌樓，華西街的傳奇老店「小王煮瓜」正在等著我們。準備好迎接米其林認證的台灣味了嗎？

Leaving serenity behind, we dive into the lively world of a night market. Pass under the iconic red archway to find our goal: "Hsiao Wang Chih Kua," a legendary, Michelin-approved taste of Taiwan.

😋點餐秘訣 / How to Order Like a Pro

  • 🍚

    香菇魯肉飯 (必點)：深色的「黑金」滷汁是它的招牌。肥瘦相間的豬肉充滿膠質，鹹甜滋味完美扒在每一粒米飯上。

    Braised Pork Rice (Must-have): The signature "black gold" sauce is iconic. Fatty pork belly provides a rich, collagen-packed, savory-sweet flavor that coats every grain of rice.

  • 🥣

    清湯瓜仔肉 (絕配)：這碗湯是魯肉飯的靈魂伴侶。醃瓜的甘甜清爽了味蕾，讓你還能再戰一碗飯！

    Pork & Cucumber Soup (Perfect Match): The soulmate to the rich rice. The pickled cucumber's sweetness cleanses your palate, making you ready for another bite.

  • 🥚

    滷鴨蛋 & 滷豆腐 (升級)：讓這些小菜吸滿濃郁的滷汁，是體驗「小王」風味宇宙的完整方式。

    Braised Egg & Tofu (Level up): Let these sides, soaked in the same amazing sauce, complete your flavor experience.

祝你有個難忘的艋舺之夜！
Have an unforgettable night in Monga!
